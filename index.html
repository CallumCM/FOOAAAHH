How to Play



You're flying your airplane carrying a huge load of honey, when suddenly your plane explodes! You're falling and need to slow down—if you go too fast, you'll pass out. Use your left and right arrow keys to move between lanes, avoiding birds and airplane parts and trying to hit flying globs of honey and clouds to slow down.
